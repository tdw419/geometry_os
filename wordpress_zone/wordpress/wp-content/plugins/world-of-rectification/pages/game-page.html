How It Works

  1. Choose Your Path: Each quest offers Easy Mode (obvious choices) or Hard Mode (contribute to Geometry OS)
  2. Earn CTRM Points: Your decisions are scored by the Cognitive Transparent Reasoning Model
  3. Level Up: As your CTRM grows, you unlock higher TMS access levels
  4. Release Sparks: Complete quests to release sparks and restore the world
