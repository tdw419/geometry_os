What is CTRM?

CTRM (Cognitive Transparent Reasoning Model) measures your reasoning quality based on:

  • Decision consistency - Do your choices align with stewardship values?
  • Reasoning depth - How well do you explain your reasoning in hard mode?
  • Contribution quality - Are your Geometry OS submissions accepted?
  • Time investment - Thoughtful decisions earn more than quick ones

TMS Access

Truth Management System (TMS) access unlocks as your CTRM grows:

  • TMS Level 1 (100 CTRM) - Save progress, earn credits
  • TMS Level 2 (250 CTRM) - Submit contributions to Geometry OS
  • TMS Level 3 (500 CTRM) - Verify other players' contributions
  • TMS Level 4 (1000 CTRM) - Run your own local WordPress node
  • TMS Level 5 (2500 CTRM) - GEMS system eligibility
