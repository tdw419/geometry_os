# Design: fix-memory-leaks-daemon

Area: Missing Core Functionality

Description:
Incomplete implementation of some critical system components that are essential for the functionality of the project. The following issues are present:

1. Integration with payment gateway providers: The platform must integrate seamlessly with multiple payment gateways to offer a complete range of payment options to our clients' customers. Currently, the project is only compatible with one payment gateway provider. This creates a significant challenge as it limits the platform's functionality and restricts customer choice.

2. Implementation of a search engine: A robust search engine must be implemented to enable customers to easily and efficiently find products based on their preference. The current search system does not meet our client's requirements and causes delay in transactions. This creates a negative impact on the platform's performance and customer satisfaction.

3. Real-time stock tracking: An efficient real-time stock tracking system is crucial for our clients to monitor inventory levels in real-time, especially during peak demand periods. Currently, we have a manual tracking system that causes delays in transactions. This creates a negative impact on the platform's performance and customer satisfaction.

Benefits:
The integration with payment gateway providers, as well as the implementation of a robust search engine, would enhance the platform's overall user experience and drive significant revenues for our client. The real-time stock tracking system would allow our clients to efficiently find products based on their preference, leading to higher conversion rates and revenue growth.

Risk:
The absence of these essential components could lead to significant challenges in implementing the platform's functionality, affecting user experience and increasing customer dissatisfaction. The integration with payment gateway providers may create delays in transactions due to manual tracking systems, resulting in increased costs for our client. In addition, a lack of real-time stock tracking would negatively impact operations by limiting inventory management efficiency.

In conclusion, the implementation of these essential system components is crucial for the functionality and success of the project. The absence of these components could lead to significant challenges in implementing the platform's functionality, affecting user experience and increasing customer dissatisfaction. The integration with payment gateway providers would enhance the platform's overall user experience and drive revenues for our client, while the implementation of a robust search engine would enable customers to easily find products based on their preference. The real-time stock tracking system would allow our clients to efficiently manage inventory levels in real-time, leading to increased revenues and satisfaction.
